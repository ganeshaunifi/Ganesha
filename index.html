Research Lines

The project is structured into four main research lines

1
Formal Network Language and Cognitive Network Surrogates

Design of a formal language and cognitive network surrogates for explicit, structured representation of network state, events, actions, and causal relations.
2
Deductive Reasoning over Network Surrogates

Development of deductive reasoning mechanisms for multi-step, logic-driven inference, including causal and counterfactual reasoning.
3
Deductive–Inductive Integration and Human-Interrogable Networks

Integration of deductive reasoning with inductive learning, enabling natural language queries and interactive cognitive partnership.
4
End-to-End Theoretical Foundations and Performance Characterization

Rigorous theoretical analysis of reasoning correctness, stability, robustness, and new metrics for reasoning quality and explainability.
